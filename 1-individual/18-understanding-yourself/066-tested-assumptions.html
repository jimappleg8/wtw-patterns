66 Tested Assumptions

If you are alert -- Active Mind (99), you will begin to identify some of the assumptions that form the core of your understanding. This pattern discusses how we can test an assuption once we identify it.

By definition, we treat assumptions as truth without questioning them, but assuptions can be wrong just like any knowledge and they need to be tested.

An assuption is a little door in our mind that will reman closed unless we make a point of opening it and examinng the facts in the room beyond. Assumptions become assuptions because somewhere along the line we became convinced that they were accurate and we stopped even looking at them.
