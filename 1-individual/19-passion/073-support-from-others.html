73 Support From Others

insert context statement

Insert problem statement.

It's important to surround yourself with people who will support the things you are trying to do. The more difficult the task, the more important this is. Friends and family who undermine your efforts will make things much more difficult for you.

It's important to note that "support" is not just simple encouragement, but it includes constructive criticism of your work. You need to listen to objections and decide if they have merit. You can't go forward blindly and surround yourself with "yes men".

Therefore:

Surround yourself with people who will support you in what you are trying to accomplish.
