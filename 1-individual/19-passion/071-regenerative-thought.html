A regenerative thought is one that creates energy in the thinker. It is akin to "positive thinking" but I really want to avoid that phrase beecause I think the common understanding of it is covered in Polyannaesque Viewpoint. I think of regenerative thought like Dewey talked about an educational experience: a regenerative thought leads to further thought. It does not close down the process of thinking. It spurs new questions and new ideas and is potentially boundless.
